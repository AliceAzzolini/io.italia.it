The services available through io

IO stems from citizens’ needs. We will work with the operators of each service for integration into the app.
To set priorities, we organized the services on the basis of how many people could actually use them, and how often they impact upon daily lives. The services you see on this site are just a few examples.
Trials at a local or national level have started for a selection of services. The more complex ones will be released in a few months or years. Undoubtedly, some possible services have been overlooked. If you think of another service that seems relevant, please make a suggestion in the appropriate section of the forum.

Roadmap

Fourth Quarter 2018

  • Involvement of stakeholders in testing

    One hundred members of the institutions and collaborators of the Digital Team were invited to test the real application on their devices. Over twenty messages of different types have been sent and credit card payment streams, either from a message or from a QR code, have been tested.

First Quarter 2019

  • Biometric identification

    IO will allow, as an alternative to the PIN, to use the biometric identification available in your device: fingerprint or facial recognition (iOS only). It will still require the choice of a PIN as a fallback.

  • Payment notices and transactions will be linked

    In the event of messages containing payment notices already paid by IO, the message will show a check mark instead of the "pay" button and a direct link to the receipt of the specific transaction.

  • More payment methods

    You will also be able to pay through Bancomat Pay, PayPal, Satispay.

  • Account cancellation

    It will be possible to completely delete all the data related to your profile on IO.

  • Export your data

    Any data related to your account can be exported in open format.

Second Quarter 2019

  • Test by invitation of a first beta version (closed-beta)

    Some local and central authorities will invite a few thousand citizens to test the app. Messages and payments will actually be sent by the institutions' systems, and will therefore be perfectly valid.

Third Quarter 2019

  • Publication of the beta version in the stores

    Anyone will be able to download IO from the Android and iOS stores. However, the launch of the application will take place on a local basis, where institutions have effectively integrated a sufficient number of services.
